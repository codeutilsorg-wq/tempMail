## üéØ Service Selection Criteria

### **When Choosing Next Service:**

**‚úÖ Must Have:**
1. **Recurring Revenue** (SaaS, subscriptions)
2. **Low Maintenance** (< 5 hours/week after launch)
3. **Complements Existing Services** (cross-sell opportunities)
4. **Proven Demand** (existing competitors making money)
5. **Technical Feasibility** (can build in 3-6 months)

**‚ùå Avoid:**
1. One-time payment models (consulting, freelancing)
2. High-touch sales (enterprise sales)
3. Highly regulated industries (fintech, healthcare)
4. Saturated markets (generic productivity tools)
5. Requires large team (can't solo/small team)

## üöÄ Success Factors

### **Critical Success Factors:**

1. **Consistency** - Don't quit during slow periods
2. **Focus** - Don't launch too many services too fast
3. **Automation** - Build systems, not jobs
4. **Leverage** - Reuse code, infrastructure, audience
5. **Patience** - Compounding takes time
6. **Pruning** - Kill underperformers ruthlessly
7. **Learning** - Each service teaches lessons for next

### **Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**

1. ‚ùå Launching too many services at once
2. ‚ùå Not giving services enough time to grow
3. ‚ùå Ignoring maintenance of existing services
4. ‚ùå Chasing shiny new ideas constantly
5. ‚ùå Not automating/delegating
6. ‚ùå Burning out from overwork
7. ‚ùå Not tracking metrics properly
