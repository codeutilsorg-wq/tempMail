Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

EasyTempInbox is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Temporary email addresses: Randomly generated, not linked to your identity
  • Incoming emails: Stored temporarily and automatically deleted after expiry
  • No personal information: We don't require registration or personal details

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Display incoming emails in your temporary inbox
  • Automatically delete emails after the expiry period
  • Maintain service functionality

3. Data Retention

All data is temporary:

  • Inboxes and emails are automatically deleted after the selected expiry time (10 minutes to 24 hours)
  • We do not create permanent records of email content
  • No backups of user data are maintained

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): For infrastructure and email processing. AWS processes data according to their privacy policies and security standards.
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and understanding visitor behavior. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies. Your session is managed entirely in your browser.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Automatic data deletion

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All temporary data is automatically removed after expiry.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@easytempinbox.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
