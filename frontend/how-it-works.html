Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free. No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no limits on usage.

Can I send emails?

No, EasyTempInbox is receive-only. You can only view incoming emails.

Is my inbox private?

Anyone with your temporary email address can view the inbox. Don't use it for sensitive information.

Can I extend the expiry time?

No, once created, the expiry time is fixed. Generate a new inbox if you need more time.
